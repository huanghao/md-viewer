1. Introduction
译 T to translate The rapid advancement of deep learning has transformed natural language processing, enabling systems to perform translation tasks with unprecedented accuracy. However, the computational cost of these systems remains a significant barrier to widespread deployment.
深度学习的快速发展改变了自然语言处理，使系统能够以前所未有的精度执行翻译任务。然而，这些系统的计算成本仍然是广泛部署的重大障碍。
译 T to translate In this paper, we introduce TransFormer-XL, a transformer-based architecture that addresses these limitations through a combination of sparse attention mechanisms and knowledge distillation. Our key contributions are threefold.

译 T to translate First, we propose a novel sparse attention pattern that reduces the quadratic complexity of standard self-attention to near-linear, while preserving translation quality on long documents. Second, we develop a cross-lingual knowledge distillation framework that transfers knowledge from a large teacher model to a compact student model.
首先，我们提出了一种新颖的稀疏注意力模式，将标准自注意力的二次复杂度降低到近线性，同时保持长文档的翻译质量。其次，我们开发了一个跨语言知识蒸馏框架，将知识从大型教师模型转移到紧凑的学生模型。
2. Related Work
译 T to translate Neural machine translation has evolved significantly since the introduction of sequence-to-sequence models. The transformer architecture (Vaswani et al., 2017) marked a turning point, replacing recurrent networks with self-attention mechanisms that allow for parallelization during training.
自序列到序列模型引入以来，神经机器翻译已经显著发展。Transformer 架构（Vaswani et al., 2017）标志着一个转折点，用允许训练期间并行化的自注意力机制替代了循环网络。
